Our Story

Family-Owned Contracting Excellence

Gauthier's Total Home Improvements is a family-owned contracting business at the heart of Seattle, rooted in faith, integrity, and a dedication to delivering quality craftsmanship.

We believe in honoring God through our service and enriching lives by transforming homes. Every project we undertake is guided by old-fashioned values combined with modern craftsmanship techniques to deliver exceptional results.

Our process includes preconstruction design, detailed cost estimates, regular on-site consultations, and final walkthroughs to guarantee customer satisfaction. We work with highly trained, licensed, and insured professionals who share our commitment to excellence.

Located in Kirkland and proudly serving Seattle, Bellevue, Redmond, and surrounding areas, we're dedicated to transforming your vision into reality.
